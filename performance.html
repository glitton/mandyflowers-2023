Performance

With impeccable time, a strong groove and a deep, beautiful sound, Mandy Flowers creates smooth, supportive bass lines and expressive, melodic solos. With both reading and improvising skills, Mandy has garnered experience in a wide range of performance types: small groups of all kinds, musicals, big band, free jazz, accompanying vocals and spoken word.
Available for local and touring performances; small group bandleader.
